About Rabbi Chess Club

Rabbi, Fostering Excellence and Growth Through Chess Programs

Rabbi Chess Club is a registered company in Kenya. We offer a variety of Chess coaching services to schools, institutions, and corporates.

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as a globally renowned provider of exceptional Chess coaching services.

Our Mission

To refine and develop every sporting talent into a proficient skill.
